How to Use the Booking Widget

  1. Select your preferred date from the calendar
  2. Enter the number of guests in your party
  3. Choose an available time slot from the options provided
  4. Select any required menu options or add-ons
  5. Choose a seating area if available
  6. Click "Proceed to Booking" to enter your details
  7. Complete the booking form with your contact information
  8. Confirm your reservation

Developer Instructions

To embed this widget on your own website:

  1. Include the Eveve widget script in your HTML file: 
    <script type="module" src="/testform25/assets/index-[hash].js"></script>
  2. Create a container element with an ID: 
    <div id="eveve-booking-widget"></div>
  3. Make sure to include the restaurant ID parameter (est) in the URL: 
    https://yourwebsite.com/booking?est=testnza
